# GreenGrocer Business Analytics Project

## Overview

This project is a complete business analytics case study for a fictional grocery company called **GreenGrocer**. It uses Python to generate sample retail data and perform multiple layers of analysis, including:

- Descriptive Analytics
- Diagnostic Analytics
- Predictive Analytics
- Business Insights & Recommendations

The goal is to simulate real-world decision-making using data from stores, customers, sales, and operations.

---

## Project Features

### Data Generation

The script automatically creates realistic sample datasets for:

- **5 Store Locations**
  - Tampa
  - Orlando
  - Miami
  - Jacksonville
  - Gainesville

- **Daily Sales Data**
  - Full year of transactions
  - Seasonal trends
  - Weekend traffic patterns
  - Department/category performance

- **Customer Data**
  - 5,000 simulated customers
  - Income, age, gender
  - Loyalty tiers
  - Spending behavior
  - Customer segments

- **Operational Metrics**
  - Annual sales
  - Profitability
  - Sales per square foot
  - Sales per staff member
  - Customer satisfaction

---

# Analytics Sections

## 1. Descriptive Analytics

Provides an overview of current business performance.

### Includes:

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Average Profit Margin
- Sales by Store
- Sales by Department
- Customer Segment Analysis
- Visual Charts

---

## 2. Diagnostic Analytics

Explains why performance is happening.

### Includes:

- Correlation analysis between store factors and revenue
- Store efficiency comparisons
- Seasonal sales trends
- Operational performance rankings

---

## 3. Predictive Analytics

Uses basic forecasting models.

### Includes:

- Linear Regression for Store Sales Prediction
- Department Growth Trends
- Future Performance Forecasting

---

## 4. Business Recommendations

Generates strategic actions based on findings.

### Example Recommendations:

- Increase marketing in top-performing stores
- Expand high-margin departments
- Optimize staffing levels
- Target premium customer segments
- Maximize seasonal demand periods
